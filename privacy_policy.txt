PRIVACY POLICY FOR H-FIRE (RESIDENT MONITOR)

Last Updated: April 09, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION
H-Fire ("we," "our," or "us") provides a Fire and Gas Leak Monitoring System. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
To provide accurate monitoring and emergency response services, we collect the following Personally Identifiable Information (PII):
- Email Address (via Clerk/Facebook/Google)
- Full Name (First, Middle, and Last Name)
- Community / Block & Lot information
- Detailed Household Address
- Geographic Location (Latitude and Longitude)
- Unique User ID (userId)

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
The information collected is used strictly for the following purposes:
- Account Authentication: To securely sign you into the app.
- Emergency Monitoring: To map your household's gas/smoke levels.
- Emergency Response: To allow administrative monitors to identify the exact location of a fire or gas leak incident.
- Push Notifications: To send real-time alerts to your device in case of danger.

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
We use the following third-party services to operate our app:
- Clerk: For secure user authentication and management.
- Supabase: For secure database storage of profiles and sensor logs.
- HiveMQ: For real-time MQTT message transfer from hardware devices.
- Facebook/Google: For optional social login capabilities.

5. DATA RETENTION AND SECURITY
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your location and profile data are stored securely in our database and are only accessible by authorized system administrators during emergency incidents.

6. DATA DELETION INSTRUCTIONS
Users have the right to request the deletion of their account and all associated personal data. To request data deletion:
- Send an email to hfire.iotsyste@gmail.com
- Use the subject line: "H-Fire Data Deletion Request"
- Include your registered email address in the body.
Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete your profile, location data, and account from our systems within 48 hours.
